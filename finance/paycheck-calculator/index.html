About This Calculator

Uses 2024 IRS federal income tax brackets and FICA rates. Standard deduction is automatically applied ($14,600 single / $29,200 MFJ / $21,900 HOH for 2024). Social Security tax is 6.2% on wages up to $168,600. Medicare is 1.45% with no income limit (additional 0.9% applies above $200K for single filers, not modeled here). State income tax is not included — add your state rate separately. Pre-tax deductions (401k, HSA, FSA) reduce your taxable income.
