How to Use This Calculator

  • Annual salary: Enter your yearly gross salary. Standard is 2,080 hours (40 hrs × 52 weeks).
  • Hourly to annual: Select "Hourly", enter your rate. Use actual hours worked per week.
  • Comparing offers: A $42/hr full-time job = $87,360/year. Use the compare tool above.
  • State tax: Florida, Texas, Washington = 0%. California ≈ 9.3%. New York City ≈ 12.7%.
