2024 Rules Summary

Expense limits: $3,000 for 1 child, $6,000 for 2+ children.

Credit rate: 20%–35% of qualifying expenses, depending on AGI. Phases down from 35% (AGI <$15K) to 20% (AGI >$43K).

FSA interaction: If you use a Dependent Care FSA, you must reduce qualifying expenses by the FSA amount. You can't double-dip.

Non-refundable: This credit reduces your tax bill to zero but won't generate a refund beyond what you owe.
