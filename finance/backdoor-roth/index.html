Pro-rata rule: If you have any pre-tax IRA money (traditional, SEP, or SIMPLE IRA), the IRS treats all your IRAs as one pool when converting. You can't just convert the after-tax portion tax-free. To avoid the pro-rata rule, roll pre-tax IRA funds into your 401(k) before doing the backdoor Roth.
