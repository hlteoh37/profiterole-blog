Hidden Costs People Miss

Most people underestimate moving costs by 30-50%. The big surprises: cleaning your old place (landlords charge $200-500+), replacing items you donated instead of moving, eating out every night during the move, and the cost of getting your new place functional (shower curtain, lightbulbs, cleaning supplies — all add up fast).

How to Save

Book movers 4-6 weeks ahead. Move on a weekday — weekend rates are 20-30% higher. Declutter before packing (less volume = lower cost). Get three quotes and negotiate. Disassemble furniture yourself before movers arrive. Check if your employer offers relocation assistance — many companies cover some or all moving costs.

Tax Deductibility

Moving expenses are generally no longer deductible for most people under current US tax law (suspended through 2025). The exception: active-duty military members moving under orders. Always check current IRS rules.
